The holograms shown here are amazing aren't they! I love them and I worked so incredibly hard on them but it was worth it dawg. I am a creative idividual with a passion for learning. One of my favorite hobbies was turning my entire room into one big elaborate Rube Goldberg machine! Nowadays I find myself doing much of the same, only this time in a laboratory! Throughout my career (both academic and professional) I've had plenty of opportunities to design, build, and program some new devices that improves our science! My hope is to continue to do more of the same in the future but I plan to go wherever my creativity takes me!
