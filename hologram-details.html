Alien Spaceship Puzzle

This is the grand finale puzzle I designed for the Area 51 themed escape room at Clue Quest (Edmond, OK). Players must collect enough fuel orbs throughout the room to power up the ship and open the door. Once the players have collected enough fuel orbs, the ship will power up enough to turn on the console and activate the holograms. There are six holograms (3 front and 3 back) that prompt the user for commands.

Five ultrasonic sensors on the console are used to select the correct symbols and progress the game. The player waves their hand over the sensor at a certain height to select a certain symbol (the aliens control their technology through telekenesis). Once solved, a platform raises to reveal the final fuel orb. Players get to experience a final system overload before the door slowly opens and they can make their escape.
