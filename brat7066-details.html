15 Segment Display Board

This is one of my very first electronics projects I took on as an adult. The mask is a Mad Max themed voice changer I made for Halloween while in graduate school. The mask uses two arduinos, one to control the lights and one to control the voice changer. The voice changer uses a WAV shield to alter your voice based on a potentiometer (high or low).
