Biochemistry Puzzle

The second major puzzle encountered by players in the area 51 themed escape room at Clue Quest (Edmomd, OK) is a biochemistry puzzle. A xeno-biochemist has locked a fuel orb for the nearby ufo behind a glass case. In order to retrieve the orb, players must learn about what the biochemist was working on. An alien autopsy has yielded several organic samples that must be analyzed.

This puzzle features 5 test tubes filled with a UV sensitive resin and an rfid tag. The test tubes are placed in the box and scanned. If in the tube is in the correct slot, the box indicates with a green LED. When all 5 test tubes are in place, UV LEDs turn on, revealing the secret color of the resin inside. The players then decode this color sequence into numbers using a switch dial, and enter the passcode into a keypad.
